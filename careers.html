Job Title: NIOS Math Teacher

Location: DLF Garden City - Thalambur OMR, Chennai

Job Description:

We are looking for a passionate Math Teacher to join our dynamic team.

Requirements:

  • Candidate must possess strong eagerness to re-learn and learn Applied Mathematics - Integers, Fractions, Geometry and Algebra.
  • Candidate must be able to apply our 30 levels of Math Games and identify talent among the children.
  • Must build models on the fly for teaching Maths - Extensive use of Papers, Card Board, Clay, and Math toys, Match Sticks, treasure hunt, etc,
  • Must develop fluency in teaching Geometry through - www.geogebra.org and develop fluency in teaching Algebra and Calculus through www.desmos.com (1 month training will be provided)
  • Must be computer familiar, to download scores from our online App, and administer assessment tests online, (EdTech)
  • Must be willing to work with the App creator to upload new questions, ideate with the team and administer short assessment sprints with the students to improve their math solving speeds. (Our App: Math Puzzle)
  • Must exhibit joy of teaching Maths. Must break down knowledge and skill well and identify natural inclination for Geometry/Arithmetic talent among children.

About the Job:

Full-time Job, requires teaching 3 batches of 8 students each in NIOS Board, time committment about 24 teaching hours a week, and 6 hours in backend analyzing scores. Initial Training will be for 2 hours a day. Good Compensation based on satisfying above requirements.
